Brian & Laura’s Amazing Wedding Adventure!

Ceremony

Our wedding will be held at Laura’s parish, St. John the Evangelist in Watertown, on June 27th, 2015 at 11:00 AM.

For driving directions, see Google Maps.

Reception

After the ceremony, there will be a reception in the Virginia Hamilton Solley Hall at The Gunnery in Washington, about 25 minutes from Watertown. For driving directions from the church, see this Google Maps link. The cocktail hour will start around 1:30 PM on the patio, and the main reception will be inside from 3:00 until 8:00.

Food & Drink

Hate deciding between chicken or beef? So does Brian; that's why it's a buffet! The entrées are pan seared chicken with lemon thyme jus; citrus & garlic grilled flank steak with chimichurri; and zucchini, fennel, spinach, and shiitake mushroom cannelloni.

Be sure to save room for a delicious wedding cake (made by Matron of Honor Michelle Sisk), as well as ice cream sundaes with Brian's and Laura's favorite flavors.

As far as drinks go, don’t worry, it’s an open bar.

Accomodations

For our guests coming in from out of town, we've reserved a block of rooms for Friday and Saturday night at the Crowne Plaza Southbury, about 20 minutes from both Watertown and Washington. Mention the O’Brien/Donorfio wedding when you call (1-888-233-9527) to get the discounted group rate, or use the link above to book online.

There are many other lodging choices in the area, including the Litchfield Inn or the Curtis House in Woodbury.

Parking

At the church: enter the shared driveway between St. John’s and Rite-Aid (on the left side of the church), and follow the driveway around the back of the church to enter the parking lot.

At The Gunnery: The Google Maps directions above will bring you to the main gate on Kirby Road. Enter the main gate and follow the signs to get to various on-campus parking areas; feel free to take any open spots, but please don’t block in any vehicles, since they might belong to faculty members who may need to leave.

Photography

We ask that you experience our wedding fully, and not just through the viewfinder on your camera. We have a fantastic photographer coming, and we’ll be sure to share pictures with everyone when they’re ready.

Also, we would like to be the first to share our photos online, and ask that you please not post photos on social networking sites until the professional photos have been shared.
